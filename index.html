We created the Belonging Barometer to explore the places and ways in which people in the U.S. find belonging.

We assess our country for its levels and “gaps” of belonging, and to identify the impact belonging—or lack of it—has on three critical dimensions of life: health, civic engagement and democracy, and intergroup relations.
